After skimming a few sites that mentioned the fracas between Scott Baio & his wife versus the internet, I would like to pass along a word of advice to the Baios, and anyone else who hold their views:

If you start an argument or conversation with..

·         I’m not racist or anything, but…

·         I’m not a homophobe, but…

·         Some of my best friends are (minority here)…

Then you’ve already lost the argument. Consider it a close relative of Godwin’s law. Thank you, drive through.

On The Painted Desert by Boom Boom Satellites Listen on Posterous

In fact, it's not really cheesy at all...

When I saw Moby in '99, touring in support of the "Play" album, Boom Boom Satellites opened for them. I really enjoyed the stuff they played live, but was deeply annoyed when I heard absolutely none of it on the album I picked up. So I didn't really give it a chance as I should. A few months later, I really sat down and listened to it, and totally fell in love with it... 

This is just one of those beautiful songs that you can imagine watching a sunrise, sunset, or the end of the world to.

I honestly cannot overstate how happy this makes me. I have such fond memories of going here with my parents, and just soaking up the magic. Even as a teenager, I couldn’t deny the wonder of this place, and it’s such a treat to see early footage, especially where you can see certain installations and attractions that I saw as a child, yet are gone now.

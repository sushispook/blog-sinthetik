It’s Friday, thus time for Friday Cheese…

 

The odds are pretty good that you’ve never heard this one before. It’s from a compilation album where everything else… does not hold up to the test of time very well at all:

 

The imagery for this always cracked me up. Skulls. For Techno. Such death imagery for something that, in 1992, was supposed to be about “Peace, Love, Unity, and Respect” kind of tells me that some graphic designer was up late and just wanted to go home – “Fuck you, Killer Techno, here’s some stock images, I’m going home now”.

Cherry Pie by Underworld Listen on Posterous

No, this isn’t a techno cover of that Warrant song. We’re just getting that right the fuck out of the way immediately, thank you very much. No, this is a B-side to Underworld’s “Pearls Girl” single. And while I’ve always dug Underworld, I’ve personally always felt their best when they’re just going all out no-vocals. Because they’re one of the few musical acts out there that can replicate the feeling of a beautiful day with friends or your loved ones with the sun on your face or the moon in the sky, without even uttering a single goddamn word.

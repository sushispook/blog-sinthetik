This was one of the first techno songs I listened to that I actually felt was stylish. It could be the backdrop for so much more than just a dance club or a rave, and it's absolutely no surprise to me that much of his work has found its way into various movie soundtracks. And it still holds up fairly well to this day, unlike so much from the early 90's.

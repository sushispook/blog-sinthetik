Emergency Broadcast Network is the only musical act that I can conceive of that could actually take Harrison Ford and make the man full of the goddamn funk:

Get_Down_Ver._2.2.mp3 Listen on Posterous

EBN is one of those acts that, in my mind, were perfect for the 90's - it's an absolute staggering mystery to me that they didn't get wider recognition and become the sort of act that's somewhere between making excellent music through a hybridization of techno, industrial, dialogue samples, and straight up funk samples, and an absolute event to see live. It's one thing to be entertaining, and it's another think to be wickedly clever. But to be both and stand out and above of much of music of the 90's, and not get the recognition they deserved? That's a crying shame. 

And now that you've listened to the song (and I recommend that you do so first), now watch the video, and understand exactly where the audio samples come from and how well they're blended (warning for some graphic imagery):

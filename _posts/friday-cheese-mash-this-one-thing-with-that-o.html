Oh the 90’s… when you could be “dangerous” and “subversive” by creating a parody of an established brand or product…

And so it was with today’s slice of techno cheese… mashing up the kid’s song “oats, peas, beans & barley” with “hellraiser”. Not bad, mind you – but it’s sort of adorably quaint now.

Halcyon (Live) by Orbital Listen on Posterous

My very first exposure to what would be called mash-ups was Information Society’s blending of PWEI’s “Ich Bin Ein Auslander” with “This Is Halloween” from the Nightmare Before Christmas Soundtrack, appropriately titled “Ausoween”. But the most memorable for me? Orbital’s live rendition of “Halcyon”, where they suddenly mix in both Bon Jovi and Belinda Carlisle.

Enjoy!

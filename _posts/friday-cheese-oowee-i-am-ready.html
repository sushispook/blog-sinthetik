Funfact: Church of Extacy was one of the many names used by the duo behind Greater Than One. 

It's funny, because when I first heard this one, it truly did sound like crazy hard-edged electronic music from another planet - just the craziest thing, and I wasn't even sure if I liked it. And once again, like so much of the music from that era, it's mellowed with age to the point where it's almost adorable in how rooted in the moment this music was.

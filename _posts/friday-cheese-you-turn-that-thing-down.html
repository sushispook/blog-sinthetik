I really wish I had it in me to write another intro/setup like I did for this particular song (I did actually creep myself out a bit writing that), but I haven't the spark nor the time to carefully shepherd one to proper ember-ship. 

For this entry, I actually never really stopped to ask myself what, exactly this song is all about. Run away/Release the body? Sometimes he doesn't believe in his victory over tyranny? Take your eyes away from him, you know he's apathetic? 

As the kids say... Da Fuq?

Sad But True by Orbital Listen on Posterous

This week’s slice of cheese comes from Orbital (in honor of the fact that they’re playing tonight). They were the first techno act that I got into that wasn’t straight up candy-rave, but wasn’t as mind-numbingly dull as most ambient wank-fests were. This particular song is one that holds up fairly well, considering that its 16 years old.

Have a listen – and enjoy.

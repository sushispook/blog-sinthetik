If hitting this site isn’t your cup of tea, there are a few different ways you can get the content…

·         RSS Feed – pretty straightforward.

·         Tumblr – Although any of the questions asked or items submitted will be posted here, and then cross-posted back over to the tumblr

·         Twitter – Short links back here are cross-posted to twitter. Fair warning: I won’t see anything @sushispook because I never log into twitter

·         Livejournal Syndication – If you’re still rocking the ElJay, the RSS feed re-posts here

·         DreamWidth Syndication – Same as above, for those of you who have jumped from ElJay

Do keep in mind that with all of the links above, there’s no filtering for any work-safe versus non-work-safe content. If there’s another location where it’s more convenient for you to get the content, please pass it along in the comments.

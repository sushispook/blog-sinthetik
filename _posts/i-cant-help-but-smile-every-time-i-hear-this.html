title: I can't help but smile every time I hear this song date: 2011/04/26 07:48:43 -0700 tags: [] author: Professor Crabwise Stabbington alias: /i-cant-help-but-smile-every-time-i-hear-this When I was a kid, my Mom rented a bunch of Ernie Kovacs videos from the local store (Video Droid, for any of you who know where I'm talking about). This would have been the late 80's, so it's not like it was topically accurate by any stretch of the imagination. But I totally fell in love with his work (especially Percy Dovetonsils), and I have super fond memories of hanging on the couch with the family, cracking up and watching his show. 

I can't help but hear that theme and smile every single time.

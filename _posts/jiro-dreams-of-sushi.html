As much as this looks like it’s going to be some serious hardcore food pornography about my favorite cuisine ever, it also looks like it’s going to be severely depressing as well, showing a life that has room for almost nothing else. I can’t call it a life not lived, because that would be presumptuous and shitty of me to do so, but what I do see makes me think that it’s an unforgiving life.

Still, I’m likely going to watch it eventually – even if it’s not in theaters, then probably via Netflix when it inevitably gets released.

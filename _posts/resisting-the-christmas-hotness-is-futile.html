title: RESISTING THE CHRISTMAS HOTNESS IS FUTILE! date: 2010/12/14 18:19:22 -0800 tags: [] author: Professor Crabwise Stabbington alias: /resisting-the-christmas-hotness-is-futile

If you’re in the bay area, and you have no plans, YOU NEED TO BE HERE. Meshuggah Beach Party – Hacidic Surf Rock Guitar – is one of the most fun live musical shows I’ve ever seen. There will be gorgeous and talented performers shaking it for your pleasure. And so what if it rains? What’s raises the temperature of your blood better than alcohol and scantily-clad men and women?

Come on out – live entertainment is one of the most wonderful experiences out there, and it keeps culture alive (and talent fed and housed!). If you support fun, vote with your feet and come on out. And if you’d rather see another pottery barn or similar analogue, then vote with your ass by sitting on it and letting NAKED FUN pass you by.

I hope to see y’all there!

title: RIP Etta James date: 2012/01/20 09:22:12 -0800 tags: [] author: Professor Crabwise Stabbington alias: /rip-etta-james

On October 14th, 2006, I watched my wife come walking down the aisle towards me. Radiant with beauty, her eyes shining with tears of joy... and against all reason and possibility, I still had just enough room left in the world of my head to listen to the music that accompanied her…

Thank you Etta. You were amazing, and not just because you added that extra joy to that wonderful day.

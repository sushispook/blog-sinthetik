Pick your soundtrack for the end of the world. It can be a song, an album, a rendition of a classical piece of music, or anything else – it just needs to be music. And to mix it up, you need to qualify it as:        

·         Being played as a soundtrack for a film showing the end of the world (bonus points for discussing exactly how the world is ending)

·         The music played during the final party you’re at as the world comes to an end (bonus points for discussing attire and preferred cocktail)

At my final party, as the sun explodes and swallows the earth?

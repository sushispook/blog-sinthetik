It's A Fine Day by Opus Iii Listen on Posterous

I remember this one getting some airplay on the local alternative radio station (alternative before it devolved into Nickelback’s descendants), and like a lot of techno in the early 90’s, they went away pretty quickly after this one hit. It’s funny, because I’ve still got the disc for this, and it’s a 2-track single – something that you don’t see at all these days, it would never sell when people could just go buy it on iTunes.
